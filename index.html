How to Use This SGPA Calculator

Select your semester.

Choose the calculation mode: Prediction or Verification.

Prediction Mode: You have to enter your ESE marks (out of 80/60) or predict those

Verification Mode: You have to verify your Result and you have to enter your Scaled ESE marks (as per result)

Enter the marks for each subject.

Click the calculate button on the semester page.

View your SGPA result.

Provide FeedBack.

How SGPA is Calculated

SGPA = (Total Credit Points Earned) ÷ (Total Credits)

Where,
Credit Points Earned = (Subject Credit) × (Grade Point) for each subject
Total Credits = Sum of credits for all subjects

Example: If a subject has 4 credits and grade point 9,
Credit Point = 4 × 9 = 36
